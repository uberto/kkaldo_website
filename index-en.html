What we are doing now

Helping a globally recognized investment bank, we are bringing to the table our experience to design an innovative software solution to track and manage the market risk. Thereby enabling more informed decision-making and improved financial outcomes.
Developing an open source library called Kondor-Json to manage the conversion of domain objects to and from JSON. What sets this library apart is its independence from reflection, annotation, or configuration - a design choice that ensures a streamlined and uncomplicated user experience. Notably, we have developed this tool to operate without any external dependencies, adding to its reliability and ease of use.
Programming on tatami: a fun and relaxed course designed for primary school children to learn programming. The aim of this course is to empower kids to create (and play) with their unique video game while simultaneously acquiring knowledge of the Lua language and the essential principles of programming.

Our story

Founded in 2023 by Uberto and Ayumi, KK Aldo set up its base in Imazu, Fukuoka - Japan.

Our name and emblem pay homage to Aldus Manutius, the Renaissance Venetian publisher, whose motto "festina lente" translates to "make haste slowly". This phrase encapsulates our ethos – blending efficiency and steadfastness to accomplish our goals.

Uberto

With decades of experience as a Software Architect, Uberto is a recognized Google Developer Expert. He wears many hats - software architect, author, blogger, and international speaker on functional programming and agile.

Ayumi

She studied Music Therapy and Environmental Technology in Germany. Ayumi manages marketing and finance at KK ALDO. She also devotes her time to discovering sustainable technologies for ecological development.
