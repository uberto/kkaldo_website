About Us

Founded in 2023 by Uberto and Ayumi, KK Aldo set up its base in Imazu, Fukuoka-Japan.

Our name and emblem pay homage to Aldus Manutius, the Renaissance Venetian publisher, whose motto "festina lente" translates to "make haste slowly". This phrase encapsulates our ethos – blending efficiency and steadfastness to accomplish a grand vision.

Uberto

With decades of experience as a Software Architect, Uberto is a recognized Google Developer Expert. He wears many hats - author, blogger, and international speaker on functional programming and agile.

Ayumi

As a music teacher and an expert in Environmental Technology, Ayumi manages marketing and finance at KK Aldo. She also devotes her time to discovering sustainable technologies for ecological development.
